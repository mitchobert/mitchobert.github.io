Course History Redesign

Project Type: Product Design

My Role: Research Prototyping Usability Testing Interaction Design Visual Design Front End

The goal of this project was to make it easier and more intuitive for students to find and send their transcripts and access their old course materials.

After looking at the analytics and conducting some research interviews, I found that students were having difficulty finding their old course grades, emails, and transcripts because they didn’t know where to look. By combining the Archives and Course History pages, I made the process more streamlined.

To make it easier for students to find what they’re looking for, I added the ability to filter their previous courses and show and hide additional detail.

A project this massive has a lot of moving parts. In cases like this utilizing Illustrator’s artboards help me quickly design multiple views of a page depending on what action the user takes. I find it easier to create a streamlined experience when you have a more holistic view of what you’re designing.

To demonstrate the functionality and responsive design, I created a quick prototype for QA and the developers.
